Dropbox Integration

The App connects to Dropbox using the official Dropbox API with your explicit authorization. By connecting your Dropbox account, you acknowledge the following:

  • The App does not transmit your Dropbox credentials to the developer or any third party. Authentication is handled entirely by Dropbox.
  • Your Dropbox connection can be revoked at any time through your Dropbox account settings at dropbox.com under Connected Apps.
  • Your use of Dropbox is subject to Dropbox's own Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.
